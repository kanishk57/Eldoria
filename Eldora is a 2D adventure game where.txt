Eldora is a 2D adventure game where the player's primary objective is to unlock a hidden treasure by exploring the game world and collecting essential keys. The journey involves engaging with various elements: defeating small green monsters that drop magical potions, encountering blue slimes that replenish the player's health, and interacting with key characters such as a wise sage (referred to as "baba") and another helper who provide the crucial keys needed to progress. The final challenge is to locate and defeat large purple slime monsters, who act as powerful gatekeepers to the treasure. The game encourages exploration, strategic combat, and interaction with non-playable characters to achieve its goal.
